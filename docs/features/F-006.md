# F-006: Autocombat Danger Core

## Objective

Characterize and extract autocombat danger checks into the portable core.

## User Value

`MonsterCanAttack` and `NearlyDead` are the remaining small dependencies at the front of the legacy `AutoCombat` decision flow. Extracting them keeps the eventual full command generator focused on macro decisions instead of hidden global state for monster reach, dragon line attacks, character health, and alive status.

## Non-Goals

- Do not port the full `AutoCombat` command generator.
- Do not port spell casting, weapon choice, healing, macro queueing, preferences, rendering, or audio.
- Do not modify legacy `Sources/`.
- Do not change behavior captured from `Sources/UltimaAutocombat.c`.

## Acceptance Notes

- Portable implementation covers `MonsterCanAttack` and `NearlyDead`, or namespaced equivalents.
- Harness tests cover adjacent monster danger, dragon diagonal attacks, non-dragon diagonal non-danger, specific-character low-HP checks, and any-party-member dead or low-HP checks.
- Character HP and alive state are explicit in portable state.
- `make -C harness test` passes.
